## Short Description (≤132 characters)

AI-powered sidebar that summarizes LinkedIn job postings instantly — title, company, role, and requirements at a glance.

## Detailed Description

Job Scout adds a clean, collapsible sidebar to every LinkedIn job posting that gives you the essentials in seconds — no more reading walls of text.

**What it shows you:**

- Job title and company (with a brief description of what the company does)
- Location / remote status
- 2–3 sentence role summary (what you'll actually be doing day-to-day)
- Tight bulleted list of expectations and qualifications (6–8 bullets max)

**How it works:**

1. Navigate to any LinkedIn job posting
2. Job Scout extracts the job details automatically
3. The sidebar appears on the right with an AI-generated summary powered by Claude

**Features:**

- Collapse/expand toggle — hide the sidebar when you don't need it
- Caches results per job so repeat visits are instant
- Works entirely in your browser — no account required
- Enable/disable toggle in the toolbar badge

**Setup:**
You'll need a free Anthropic API key (anthropic.com). Enter it once in the extension's options page and you're done.

**Privacy:**
Job text is sent only to Anthropic's API to generate the summary. Your API key is stored locally and never shared. No analytics, no tracking. See the full privacy policy for details.
